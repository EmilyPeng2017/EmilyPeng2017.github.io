About

I work on Firmware FTL Development at Micron as a software engineer. I received my Master degree from the Dept. Electrical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2019. I was studying and working in the Printed Electronics & Flexible Intergration Lab, supervised by Prof. Xiaojun Guo.

It was a very nice weather today, so I went for a 10 km run in Brugge. Running always helps me clear my mind and feel more energetic.

My right leg was not feeling very well during the run, so I had to stop a few times to stretch it. But overall, I felt good and enjoyed the scenery along the way. I saw some beautiful flowers and trees, and a few ducks swimming in a pond.

<!-- Individual Images -->
I went to the morning market in the city center and bought some fresh fruits and vegetables after running. Along the way home, I passed by a small garden with two little cows which I saw them growing up since I moved to Bruges. They are so cute and friendly, I couldn't resist taking some pictures of them and feeding them some strawberries I bought from the market.

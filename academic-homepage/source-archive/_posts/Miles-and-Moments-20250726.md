Every time I visit Cambridge, I feel a deep sense of relaxation and happiness. I think the main reason is simple: the person I love the most is there. I love the city, its people, and the unique atmosphere that always makes my heart feel light.

Two highlights this time:

1. **Attending the Summer Music Festival held by ARM**: A huge thank you to ARM—not just in Cambridge, but also in Shanghai—for always creating the best working environment and hosting the most wonderful events for employees and their families.

The Summer Music Festival took place on the beautiful grounds of a hotel, with live music, a variety of food stalls, and the fanciest feature of all: a Ferris wheel! 🎡 We played ping-pong, Whac-A-Mole, and even tried tabletop curling. The whole atmosphere was filled with laughter, music, and warmth. It was such a joy to relax, connect, and simply enjoy the moment.

I also got to meet some of my husband's colleagues and their families. It was lovely catching up with Jack and Brother Long again! So much love for you all!! 💕 Oh, and the chocolate ice cream? It was so good I couldn’t help going back for a second scoop. (^_^)

2. **Picking raspberries**: I was so excited to find out that raspberries were in season! Now I finally understand why Raspberry Pi was named after this fruit—raspberries are just that amazing. 🍓

While cycling through the countryside, we came across countless raspberry bushes lining the road. We couldn’t resist stopping to pick some, and of course, I ate them right on the spot—sweet, juicy, and sun-warmed. It was such a simple but beautiful moment: riding through the green fields of Cambridge, picking wild raspberries, and sharing smiles with my husband.

Still feeling a little dizzy—it's been a while. But spring is always glorious, and Brugge never loses its charm.  

<!-- more -->

As I was running, I started to understand more and more why my dear colleague, GDR, enjoys bird spotting. Europe, especially Brugge, is truly home to so many birds.  

<div style="display: flex; justify-content: center; flex-wrap: wrap; gap: 20px; margin-bottom: 20px;"> 
<img src="/img/blogs/21_running/2025-03-29 110035.jpg" alt="running record" width="400"> 
</div>

These lively creatures, some tiny, some astonishingly large, each have their own unique beauty.  

After my run, I had a video call with my family. It was a special moment—the first time I played the ukulele in front of my adorable niece and my whole family. I sang *红日*, my favorite Cantonese song.  

I miss you.

Today is International Goddess Day, and I couldn’t think of a better way to celebrate than by reaching out to all my incredible girlfriends. These women are my rock, my inspiration, and my biggest cheerleaders.
<!-- more -->
I feel so proud and deeply moved to have them in my life. They are kind, dedicated, and resilient. They are true goddesses in every sense of the word. Thank you for walking alongside me on this journey called life. 💖

The weather today was absolutely perfect—sunny, warm, and just the right amount of breeze. My flowers are in full bloom, and they’re absolutely stunning! 🌸 Even though I was away in England for a week, they’ve continued to grow and thrive. I like to think they’re the best flowers ever (haha), but maybe I’m just a little biased.

On my walk today, I had the most delightful encounter with two cats. The yellow one was a bit shy, maybe even a little afraid of me. But the chubby black cat was an absolute charmer! He strutted right up to me, demanding attention, and even purred loudly (HUHUHU!) as I petted him. 🐾 It was such a heartwarming moment. I’ve already come up with Chinese names for them in my head.

What a lovely day!

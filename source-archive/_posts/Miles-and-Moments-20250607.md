I've been thinking a lot, not only about research, but about mindset. What kind of things I really want to do? What is my original dream or motivation? Will these kind of things be changed easily by someone else or the environment? And also feel a lot of kind support around me. I know I'm alone and to some extent, I even enjoy my loneliness. But still, there are some kind people who understand me and I can feel the true friendship. I mean, although this is not something I pursue.

It’s been a busy stretch—lots of meetings and presentations—but I finally completed SEM training, something I’ve been meaning to do for over a year. There have been frustrating moments, but most of the time I’ve felt focused and productive. I’m grateful again—for the growth, the challenges, and the quiet belief that things are moving in the right direction.

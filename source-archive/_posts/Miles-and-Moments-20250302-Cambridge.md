I decided to run to **Electrical Engineering Division Building** of Cambridge. Then I did.
<div style="text-align: center;"> <img src="/img/blogs/15_running/ForrestGump.jpg" alt="I just felt like running!" width="400"> </div>

<!-- more -->
I passed by **River Cam**, **King's College**, and somewhere I've never been. When I arrived, two Chinese students in Cambridge coats entered the building. I followed them in, feeling like a curious explorer. Inside, I spotted posters of professors and researchers I recognized, their work lining the walls.  It was a nice sunday.

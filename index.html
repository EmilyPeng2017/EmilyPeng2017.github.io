About Me

I am a Ph.D. researcher at KU Leuven in Belgium, working on embedded systems, wearable devices, smart sensors, and soft robotics. My long-term goal is to develop sensing technologies that can make a real difference in healthcare and rehabilitation.

Before starting my Ph.D., I worked at Microsoft and Micron. This website is a research portfolio, a publication record, and a notebook for writing about research, reading, learning, and life.

how to write your motivation letter?

Posted on 2022-12-22 Edited on 2022-12-24 In Application Views: Symbols count in article: 455 Reading time ≈ 1 mins.
This is a note

  1. For different job, do enough research and write different motivation letter. Some people like to write only one general letter and use it for all applications which is awful.

  2. Be sincere, write down your own story. Do not be boastful or unrealistic. Do not care much about using complicated sentences.

  3. Usually one page is enough. Professors or committee normally have no time to scan a long letter. Make sure your first paragraph is impressive.
